Berserk to Return

Kentaro Miura, the world-renowned manga artist, died in 2021 at age 54 and left his ongoing series, Berserk, unfinished.

Now, Young Animal -- the comic book publisher that serialized Berserk -- has announced that Berserk will continue under the supervision of Kouki Mori, a close friend of Miura and a manga artist in his own right.
